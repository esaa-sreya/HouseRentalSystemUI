Pearl House

02-07-2020
Signia Pearl, Maharashtra, India
NRS 46000000

Designed as a residential development, the project comprises of luxurious venetian styled apartments which are placed next to each other along the length of the building. The linearity of the building is accentuated by the deck and balconies that run along each room of the apartment, thereby portraying an interesting play of light and shadow on the facade. The expansive living area allows smooth cross ventilation and a spectacular 2-sided view of the bustling Bandra Kurla Complex (BKC).

The amenities include children's play area and an internationally designed landscaped garden, grand entrance lobby, temperature-controlled swimming pool, multi-purpose hall, indoor games zone and virtual golf, gymnasium, basement car parking, digital security and surveillance system, maintained by international property consultants.
