Explore luxury homes in India, including villas, bungalows, apartments, waterfront and more. Inspect luxury homes, mansions, and villas for sale worldwide in one simple search.

So when it comes to modern houses, views, light, and outdoor space are at the top of every buyer’s wish list. Oversize windows, glass walls, and open layouts are integral design elements, as are covered porches, decks for entertaining, and seamless indoor-outdoor spaces. People can’t help but come together in the great room, but they are also an efficient use of space that helps keep things bright inside.
