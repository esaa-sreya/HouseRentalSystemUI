Bandra House

01-01-2020
Bandra, Maharashtra, India
NRS 14800000

Designed as a residential development, the project comprises of luxurious venetian styled apartments which are placed next to each other along the length of thebuilding. The linearity of the building is accentuated by the deck and balconies that run along each room of the apartment, thereby portraying an interesting play of light and shadow on the facade. The expansive living area allows smooth cross ventilation and a spectacular 2-sided view of the bustling Bandra Kurla Complex (BKC).

The amenities include children's play area and an internationally designed landscaped garden, grand entrance lobby, temperature controlled swimming pool, multi-purposehall, indoor gaming zone and virtual golf, gymnasium, basement car parking, digital security and surveillance system, maintained by international property consultants.
