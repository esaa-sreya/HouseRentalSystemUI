Anandapur House

13-04-2020
Anandapur, West Bengal, India
NRS 29000000

Urbana, home to Kolkata’s tallest towers is built amidst 66 acres of luxury in the middle of the city where one can enjoy a truly exquisite and opulent living experience. Located on the 44th & 45th floor, this magnificent duplex for sale in Kolkata has a breath taking 360-degree view of the greens within the township and rolling farmlands beyond. The Penthouse comes with a swimming pool and a deck and has central VRF air-conditioning for all spaces spread over two levels. The central living space has a double-height ceiling and gorgeous views of the Kolkata skyline with large glazed windows. The Duplex comes with 2 staff quarters with attached washrooms as well.

‘Club Urbana’ is a five-star facility built over 1,00,000 sq. ft. with a large infinity pool, steam & sauna rooms, exclusive multi-gym and restaurants and elegant community halls for occasions. A well-stocked convenience store, 24x7 power back, smoke detectors and fire alarms, high-speed lifts, water treatment facility, rainwater harvesting make this development a truly world-class facility to reside in. Minutes away are the city’s top schools, prime healthcare options, and a plethora of shopping & entertainment destinations nearby.
