Mayfair House

06-06-2020
Mayfair Gardens, India
NRS 33900000

Nestled in the peaceful residential locality of South Delhi, Mayfair Gardens is a posh haven with an exclusive and limited number of residents. This Basement & Ground Floor which is connected via a modern lift and a staircase, is a duplex property with a classic and stunning, marble and wood aesthetic running uniformly through the house with elegant railings all over. It spans over a built-up area of around 6,200 sq. ft., within a 630 sq. yd plot which faces a beautiful park.

The house for sale in South Delhi has 4 spacious bedrooms with impressive & well-equipped ensuite bathrooms with storage solutions, an abundant living & dining area, a well-furnished kitchen with advanced equipment, a family lounge and a separate powder room.The floor-to-ceiling windows make for a warm and comfortable ambience as they bring in plenty of sunlight and add to the opulence and grandeur of the interior. The basement on the other hand has great scope for decoration and has an independent kitchenette. It is as well suited for an office space as it is to host gatherings and it also has a complete bathroom. The house is ready to move in and comes with five covered parking spaces on the stilt level. The residents of this particular apartment get access to a private entrance.
