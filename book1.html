Kucha House

02-04-2020
Kucha Chalan, Delhi, India
NRS 1300000

Overlooking the lush greens of Rajghat, Shakti Sthal and Shanti Van; this North facing bungalow in Daryaganj is centrally located in Delhi. Old, Central, South, East Delhi and Noida is 10 to 20 minutes drive. The area is in close proximity to well-connected metro stations, and most popular schools, colleges, markets, hotels, hospitals and railway stations. Moments away are the chic boutiques, restaurants, cafés and market of Connaught Place; moreover, business and employment opportunities in central business district (CBD) Connaught Place make this one of the city’s most desirable areas. An ideal locality for publishers, businessmen or professionals working in Connaught Place or for professionals like Chartered Accountants, Lawyers or Architects to set up an office cum residence.

The bungalow sits on 1,276 sq. yds. plot with a built-up area of 10,000 sq. ft., spread across 4 levels - basement, ground, first and second floor. The residence consist of 10 bedrooms, staff quarters, and has ample parking space in front of the property The property is still with the family of original allottee, with no change of hands in the past 88 years, and is attractively priced, which makes it a preferred buy for the prospective buyer.
